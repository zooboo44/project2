1. Compile program
	g++ main.cpp -o output
2. Enter the number of people in the group
3. Enter the duration of the event you want to plan. First the hours and minutes in the format HH:MM
4. Enter the work period of person 1. First the hours then the minutes in the format HH:MM
5. Enter the busy schedule of person 1. First the hours then the minutes in the format HH:MM. Type -1 to quit entering events
6. Repeat for the rest of the group members
7. Observe output. The console will output a list of available times
